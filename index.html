Alert Specifications

Thresholds: Upper Fifteenmile Creek (Pine Creek, Moody): 22°C / 72°F. Lower Fifteenmile Creek (Kaser, 15/8 Mile, Eightmile): 23°C / 73°F.
Alert issued: When 2 or more sites are predicted to exceed their respective temperature threshold for 2 or more consecutive forecast days.
Minimum duration: Once issued, the alert remains active for a minimum of 5 days regardless of whether predicted temperatures drop below thresholds.
Alert called off: After the 5-day minimum has elapsed, the alert is cancelled when all three conditions are met: (1) at least 5 days have passed since the alert was first issued, (2) the current day's predicted temperatures at all sites are below their thresholds, and (3) the forecast shows 2 or more sites below threshold for 2 or more consecutive days, confirming the heat event is ending.
